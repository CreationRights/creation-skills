# **Section 1: Engagement Overview**

## **Eyebrow**

THE ENGAGEMENT MODEL

## **Headline**

Start with the system. Then build what the team needs.

## **Body**

Creation Skills engagements can begin with a C/Skills Review or a focused transformation brief.

Some clients need a full operating model before platform adoption.

Others need an agent operating blueprint, a governance reset, or a pilot around one real production path.

The work is practical.

The deliverables are designed for use.

## **Engagement Cards**

### **C/Skills Review**

A focused review of one production path, including team roles, production-agent use, rights review gaps, approval paths, tool stack, asset lifecycle, operating gaps, and Creation Rights readiness.

### **Operating Model Design**

A future-state model for how creative work should move from brief to production, agent-supported preparation, rights review, human approval, publication, reuse, reporting, and audit.

### **Agent Operating Blueprint**

Company-owned production-agent roles, operating files, prompt libraries, permission rules, token limits, blocked tasks, human checkpoints, and escalation paths.

### **Governance Blueprint**

Decision rights, approval gates, metadata requirements, NILP® checks, rights review triggers, reuse rules, risk controls, and exception paths.

### **Pilot Transformation**

A focused pilot that proves the new model on one real production path, such as release assets, project production, product media, social cutdowns, localization, documentary production, or broadcast packaging.

### **Implementation Office**

Ongoing rollout support, owner guidance, agent supervision, operating-file updates, adoption tracking, stakeholder alignment, and Creation Rights readiness.
