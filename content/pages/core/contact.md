# **Section 4: For C/Skills Review Requests**

## **Eyebrow**

C/SKILLS REVIEW

## **Headline**

Ready to review a real production path?

## **Body**

If your company already knows where the process is breaking, use the C/Skills Review page instead of the general contact form.

A C/Skills Review is for teams that want to examine how creative work moves through people, files, production agents, approval paths, rights checks, reuse rules, delivery, and records.

## **Review Triggers**

Production agents are being used without shared rules.

Prompts and examples live with individuals instead of the company.

Approval paths are unclear.

Rights review happens too late.

Talent, NILP®, or reuse questions appear after production starts.

Files move through too many informal handoffs.

The company wants Creation Rights but needs the internal process ready first.

## **CTA**

Book a C/Skills Review

# **Section 8: Confidentiality Note**

CONFIDENTIALITY

## **Headline**

Do not send sensitive assets through the general form.

## **Body**

For the first message, describe the process and the question.
