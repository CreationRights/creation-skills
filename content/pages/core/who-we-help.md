# **Section 11: Common Problems We Solve**

## **Eyebrow**

COMMON PATTERNS

## **Headline**

The problems look different by industry. The breaks are often the same.

## **Problem Cards**

### **Scattered Tools**

Teams test tools without shared rules, ownership, or review paths.

### **Loose Prompts**

Prompts sit with individuals instead of becoming company-owned operating files.

### **Broken Handoffs**

Files, formats, owners, and next steps are unclear.

### **Late Rights Review**

Rights, talent, usage, and reuse checks happen after production instead of inside the production path.

### **Unclear Approval Gates**

Nobody knows exactly who approves what or when work can move forward.

### **Agent Outputs Without Owners**

Agents generate useful prep, but no one owns the review path, escalation, or final decision.

### **Reuse Without Controls**

Old assets are refreshed, localized, repackaged, or re-posted without clear usage checks.

### **Platform Not Ready**

The company wants Creation Rights, but the internal process is not yet ready to run through it.
