# **Section 1: Why Records Matter**

## **Eyebrow**

WHY IT MATTERS

## **Headline**

A media asset without context becomes harder to govern.

## **Body**

A finished file can look complete while the record around it is incomplete.

The image is approved, but no one knows for which channel.  
The creator video is delivered, but paid usage is unclear.

# **Section 2: What a Protected Record Is**

## **Eyebrow**

PROTECTED RECORD

## **Headline**

A protected record is the context around sensitive media movement.

## **Body**

A protected record is not just metadata.

It is the record that explains why a piece of media can move, who approved it, what rights apply, what usage is allowed, and what needs to be governed.

Creation Rights can carry protected records around rights, NILP®, consent, talent use, metadata, approvals, audit, payouts, and compliance-sensitive media activity.

Creation Skills prepares the company flow so those records can exist in the first place.
