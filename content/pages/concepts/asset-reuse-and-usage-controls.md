# **Section 1: Why Reuse Controls Matter**

## **Eyebrow**

WHY IT MATTERS

## **Headline**

The first approval may not cover the next use.

## **Body**

An asset can be fully approved for one use and still not be ready for another.

A post approved for organic social may not be approved for paid media.  
A model image approved for one territory may not be approved for another.  
A product claim approved for a PDP may not be approved for a paid ad.  
A broadcast clip approved for editorial use may not be approved for sponsor use.  
A creator asset approved for one campaign may not be approved for future reuse.

Without clear reuse controls, teams rely on memory, assumptions, message threads, or speed.

Creation Skills helps companies turn reuse into a clear decision path.

# **Section 8: Production Agents and Reuse**

## **Eyebrow**

PRODUCTION AGENTS

## **Headline**

Agents can prepare reuse review. They should not approve reuse.

## **Body**

Production agents can help teams understand the reuse context.

They can summarize the original asset, flag missing fields, prepare review notes, compare new use against approved use, and route the asset to the right owner.

But agents should not decide that reuse is allowed.
