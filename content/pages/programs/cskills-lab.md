# **Program Page: C/Skills Lab**

## **Eyebrow**

C/SKILLS LAB

## **Headline**

Design your media skills hands-on.

## **Body**

The C/Skills Lab is a hands-on design environment where your team builds approved media skills, creation flows, operating files, and approval gates with Creation Skills guidance. The Lab moves from documented gaps to working skill definitions that your team can own, test, and refine before rollout.
