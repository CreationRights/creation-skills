# **Program Page: C/Skills Audit**

## **Eyebrow**

C/SKILLS AUDIT

## **Headline**

Start with a clear view of your creation flow.

## **Body**

Before a company redesigns its media creation process, it needs a clear view of how the work actually moves. The C/Skills Audit gives your team that view. It identifies where production is strong, where the creation flow breaks, where agents need skill definitions, where approval paths are unclear, where rights review arrives too late, and what needs to be ready before Creation Rights can carry the protected record.
