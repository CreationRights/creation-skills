C/SKILLS ACADEMY

## **Headline**

Transfer governed creation skills to your team.

## **Body**

The C/Skills Academy delivers structured learning programs that transfer governed media creation skills to your creative, brand, legal, production, and operations teams. The Academy builds internal capability so your team can design, operate, and improve media skills, creation flows, and approval gates independently.
