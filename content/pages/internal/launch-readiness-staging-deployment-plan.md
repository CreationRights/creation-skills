# **Creation Skills Internal Page: Launch Readiness, Staging, and Deployment Plan**

## **Page Identity**

**Creation Skills**  
**C/Skills.**  
**A Creation Rights Division**

## **Document Type**

Internal launch operations document

## **Public Status**

Not a public page

## **Purpose**

This page defines the launch process for the Creation Skills website.

The goal is to move from master document to staging to public launch without exposing unfinished pages, legal placeholders, broken forms, internal planning notes, or incomplete routes.

The launch should feel premium, controlled, and ready for serious enterprise visitors.

---

# **1\. Launch Principle**

## **Headline**

Launch only the controlled version.

## **Body**

Creation Skills has a large content library, but the public site should launch with the focused launch set.

The master document should remain complete.

The first public website should remain selective.

The build should not expose phase two pages, audience detail pages, internal planning documents, or legal placeholders that have not been approved.

## **Core Rule**

The launch site should include only approved public pages, required utility pages, working forms, and safe confirmation routes.

## **Eyebrow**

## **Eyebrow**

No confidential-material upload.  
No credential collection.  
No legal-document collection.  
No private-link request.  
No sensitive personal data request.

# **12\. Rollback Plan**

## **Eyebrow**

ROLLBACK

## **Headline**

Have a simple rollback path.

## **Body**

If the production launch has a major issue, the team should know how to return to the prior stable version.

## **Rollback Triggers**

Forms are not working.  
Legal pages are missing.  
Internal pages are exposed.  
Wrong brand lockup is live.  
Sensitive placeholder content is visible.  
Navigation is broken.  
Production domain is misconfigured.  
Analytics or cookie setup is incorrect.  
Major mobile issue appears.  
Security or privacy issue appears.

## **Rollback Actions**

Pause promotion.  
Revert to prior stable deployment.  
Disable broken form if needed.  
Remove exposed internal routes.  
Fix issue in staging.  
Re-test affected pages.  
Re-deploy after approval.
