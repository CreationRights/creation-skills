## **Purpose**

This page tracks every Creation Skills page by type, launch status, public status, recommended URL, and review needs.

The master document should preserve the full content library.

The website build should use this index to decide what launches now, what stays hidden, what requires legal review, and what can be published later.

# **1\. Status Key**

## **Launch Status**

### **Launch**

Build and publish in the first public site.

### **Utility Launch**

Build and publish for legal, trust, form, accessibility, or site function.

### **Conditional Utility Launch**

Build only if the related form or feature is live.

### **Optional Launch**

Publish only if the design and content are polished enough.

### **Phase Two**

Publish after launch as core concept depth, SEO support, or sales support.

### **Phase Three**

Publish after launch as audience-specific sales and SEO pages.

### **Phase Four**

Publish later as ecosystem, partner, speaking, or broader resource expansion.

### **Internal Only**

Keep in the master document. Do not publish.

### **Legal Review Required**

Do not publish until counsel has reviewed the page.
