# **Creation Skills Internal Page: Legal Review Tracker and Counsel Handoff**

## **Page Identity**

**Creation Skills**  
**C/Skills.**  
**A Creation Rights Division**

## **Document Type**

Internal legal review planning document

## **Public Status**

Not a public page

## **Purpose**

This page tracks the Creation Skills pages, language, forms, notices, and claims that should be reviewed by counsel before publication.

Creation Skills is a consulting and transformation division of Creation Rights. It works around media creation processes, production agents, operating files, human approval gates, rights review, NILP® checks, protected records, metadata, asset reuse, and Creation Rights readiness.

Because some pages reference rights, NILP®, consent, talent usage, protected records, privacy, cookies, and legal boundaries, legal review should happen before launch where required.

---

# **1\. Legal Review Principle**

## **Headline**

Process design language should stay separate from legal advice.

## **Body**

Creation Skills can describe process design, review points, approval gates, operating files, escalation paths, protected record models, and Creation Rights readiness.

Creation Skills should not present itself as legal counsel.

Legal decisions remain with the client and its advisors.

## **Core Legal Boundary**

Creation Skills supports process design. It does not replace legal counsel. Legal decisions remain with the client and its advisors.

## **Rule**

Every rights-sensitive page should include clear boundary language.
