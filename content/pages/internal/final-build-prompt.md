# **2\. Final Gemini Design Prompt**

## **Prompt**

Design a premium standalone website system for **Creation Skills**, branded as **C/Skills. A Creation Rights Division**.

Creation Skills is the consulting and transformation division of Creation Rights. It helps media, entertainment, brand, agency, creative operations, legal, rights, platform, and executive teams prepare the company flow behind modern media creation.

The site should feel like a creative industry consulting practice.

It should not feel like:

* A generic SaaS dashboard  
* A law firm  
* A public course provider  
* A generic AI consulting firm  
* A corporate Deloitte-style advisory site  
* A stock-photo business website

## **Visual Direction**

Create a lime green creative lab identity with a premium editorial feel.

The design should be:

* Bold  
* Creative  
* Premium  
* Structured  
* Readable  
* Enterprise polished  
* Creative-industry native  
* Slightly underground and editorial, but still serious

Use:

* Lime green as the core identity color  
* Deep charcoal or near-black text areas  
* Warm white or soft neutral backgrounds  
* Sharp modular cards  
* Editorial spacing  
* Large confident typography  
* Thin linework  
* Annotation marks  
* Production-system diagrams  
* Review gate visuals  
* Agent node patterns  
* Asset flow motifs  
* Metadata and record-inspired details  
* Cut lines, file states, routing marks, and approval stamps

Avoid:

* Generic AI gradients  
* Stock corporate people  
* Random robot imagery  
* Law firm design language  
* University or course branding  
* Overly polished corporate consulting clichés  
* Blue enterprise SaaS palette  
* Heavy black and green Deloitte imitation  
* Busy backgrounds that hurt readability

## **Logo Lockup**

Use exactly:

C/Skills.  
A Creation Rights Division

No typo.  
No extra period after the division line.  
Keep the lockup crisp, premium, and balanced.
