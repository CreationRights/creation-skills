# **Creation Skills Internal Page: Final QA Checklist**

## **Page Identity**

**Creation Skills**  
**C/Skills.**  
**A Creation Rights Division**

## **Document Type**

Internal QA checklist

## **Public Status**

Not a public page

## **Purpose**

This checklist is used before the Creation Skills site is built, reviewed, or launched.

It checks brand consistency, page completeness, navigation, legal placeholders, forms, accessibility, SEO, and the relationship between Creation Skills, Creation Rights, and CreativeIP.org.

The goal is to make sure the site feels premium, focused, and ready for serious enterprise buyers.

## **Headline**

Check the Creation Skills identity.

## **Checklist**

C/Skills. is written correctly.

A Creation Rights Division is written correctly.

No extra period after “A Creation Rights Division.”
