## **Partner Areas**

### **Legal and Rights Advisors**

For contracts, consent, NILP®, talent usage, rights review, product claims, usage scope, and legal decision-making.

### **Technology and Platform Partners**

For DAM, MAM, metadata, access controls, integrations, review tools, agent environments, and platform implementation.

### **Agent and Automation Builders**

For company-owned production agents, prompt systems, operating-file integration, access limits, review queues, and controlled agent environments.

### **Production and Post Partners**

For editing, localization, accessibility, campaign production, product media, social packaging, and delivery packages.

### **Creative Operations Advisors**

For intake, production paths, approvals, delivery, asset lifecycle, owner guidance, and adoption.

### **Risk and Insurance Partners**

For evidence, records, review practices, audit support, controls, and insurability around media production.

### **Education and Standards Partners**

For shared literacy around creative IP, rights governance, NILP®, metadata, and recognised standards.
