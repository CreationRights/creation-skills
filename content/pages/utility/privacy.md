## **Headline**

How Creation Skills handles information.

## **Subhead**

This Privacy Notice explains how Creation Skills may collect and use information from website visitors, enquiry forms, C/Skills Review requests, briefing requests, and partnership enquiries.

## **Body**

Creation Skills works with companies around media creation processes, production agents, operating files, approval gates, rights review, records, and Creation Rights readiness.

Because those conversations may involve sensitive business context, we ask visitors not to submit confidential assets, contracts, private talent materials, credentials, unreleased media, or sensitive rights notes through public forms.

This page explains the general privacy approach for the Creation Skills website.

This page should be reviewed by legal counsel before publication.

# **Section 4: C/Skills Review Requests**

## **Eyebrow**

REVIEW REQUESTS

## **Headline**

Review requests should describe the process, not expose sensitive materials.

## **Body**

A C/Skills Review request may ask for information about a production path, team structure, agent use, approval gaps, rights review questions, reuse issues, record gaps, or Creation Rights readiness.

For the first request, describe the company process and the issue.

Do not submit confidential source files, unreleased media, contracts, legal documents, private talent materials, credentials, private download links, or sensitive rights notes through public forms.

If sensitive materials are needed later, Creation Skills can discuss an agreed client process before materials are shared.
