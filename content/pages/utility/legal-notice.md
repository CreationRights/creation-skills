Three connected roles. Different jobs.

Creation Skills, Creation Rights, and CreativeIP.org are connected, but each has a different role.

## **Ecosystem Split**

### **Creation Skills**

The consulting and transformation layer.

Creation Skills prepares the company flow, production paths, production agents, operating files, human approval gates, rights review points, protected records, and Creation Rights readiness.

### **Creation Rights**

The platform layer.

The education and standards layer.

## **Eyebrow**

LEGAL BOUNDARY

## **Headline**

Creation Skills supports process design. It does not replace legal counsel.

## **Body**

Creation Skills may help companies define review points, approval gates, operating files, escalation paths, record requirements, protected record models, and Creation Rights readiness.

Creation Skills does not provide legal advice through this website.

Legal decisions remain with the client and its legal advisors.

Visitors should consult qualified counsel for legal advice related to rights, NILP®, consent, talent usage, licensing, contracts, compliance, privacy, regulatory obligations, or disputes.

NO CLIENT RELATIONSHIP

## **Headline**

Using this website, submitting a contact form, requesting a C/Skills Review, requesting a briefing, or sending a partnership enquiry does not create a client relationship.
