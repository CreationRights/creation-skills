# **Section 1: What Happens Next**

## **Eyebrow**

NEXT STEPS

## **Headline**

What happens after a review request.

## **Steps**

### **1\. Request Review**

Creation Skills reviews the information submitted.

### **2\. Fit Check**

If the request is a fit, we follow up to clarify the production path, scope, stakeholders, and timing.

### **3\. Review Setup**

If both sides decide to proceed, we agree what process will be reviewed and what representative materials may be useful.

### **4\. C/Skills Review**

Creation Skills reviews how the selected production path moves through people, production agents, approval gates, rights review, reuse, records, and Creation Rights readiness.
