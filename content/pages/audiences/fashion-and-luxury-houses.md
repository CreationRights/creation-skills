# **Section 4: Company-Owned Production Agents**

## **Eyebrow**

PRODUCTION AGENTS

## **Headline**

Fashion agents need context, limits, and review paths.

## **Body**

Production agents can help fashion and luxury teams prepare campaign and product materials faster.

They can draft product descriptions, summarize collection notes, prepare lookbook copy, create market versions, support editorial captions, organize showroom materials, and prepare social packages.

But they need rules before they support real brand media.

A production agent should know what it can prepare, what it cannot decide, who owns the output, and when a human must approve.

## **Example Agents**

### **Lookbook Draft Agent**

Prepares lookbook copy, collection notes, product story drafts, and structure options from approved materials.

### **Runway Recap Agent**

Prepares show recap drafts, collection summaries, social copy, and press support materials.

### **Product Detail Agent**

Prepares product descriptions, feature notes, PDP copy, and retail story options for review.

### **Editorial Caption Agent**

Prepares caption drafts, tone options, campaign copy, and platform-specific post structures.

### **Campaign Variant Agent**

Prepares market versions, channel copy, paid media variants, and campaign delivery notes.

### **Showroom Copy Agent**

Prepares buyer-facing copy, line notes, product education, and showroom material drafts.

### **Market Asset Agent**

Prepares localized copy drafts, territory notes, market-specific captions, and review materials.
