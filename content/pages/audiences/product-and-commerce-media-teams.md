# **Section 4: Company-Owned Production Agents**

## **Eyebrow**

PRODUCTION AGENTS

## **Headline**

Commerce agents need product facts, limits, and approval paths.

## **Body**

Production agents can help product and commerce teams prepare media faster.

They can draft PDP copy, outline product videos, prepare image briefs, create marketplace versions, summarize features, build ad variants, and check delivery packages.

But they need rules before they support real product media.

A production agent should know what it can prepare, what product information is approved, what claims require review, what it cannot decide, who owns the output, and when a human must approve.

## **Example Agents**

### **Product Video Outline Agent**

Prepares product video structures, feature notes, shot ideas, social cutdown options, and delivery checklist materials.

### **Product Image Brief Agent**

Prepares image requirements, visual references, product detail notes, channel requirements, and review-ready shot lists.

### **PDP Copy Agent**

Prepares product descriptions, feature copy, benefit statements, format notes, and review materials.

### **Marketplace Versioning Agent**

Prepares retailer-specific versions, marketplace copy, channel formats, and delivery notes.

### **Feature Breakdown Agent**

Prepares feature summaries, product education copy, comparison structures, and product-owner review materials.

### **Ad Variant Agent**

Prepares paid media copy options, format notes, channel variants, and review flags.

### **Commerce Refresh Agent**

Prepares refresh notes, SKU updates, reused asset checks, seasonal copy drafts, and channel-ready materials.
