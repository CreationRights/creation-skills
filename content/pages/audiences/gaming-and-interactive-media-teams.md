# **Section 4: Company-Owned Production Agents**

## **Eyebrow**

PRODUCTION AGENTS

## **Headline**

Sports agents need speed, limits, and approval paths.

## **Body**

Production agents can help sports and live media teams prepare content quickly.

They can summarize event notes, suggest highlights, draft captions, prepare sponsor versions, organize athlete content, build recap packages, and check delivery materials.

But they need rules before they support real sports media.

A production agent should know what it can prepare, what it cannot decide, who owns the output, and when a human must approve.

## **Example Agents**

### **Highlight Prep Agent**

Prepares clip suggestions, play notes, caption drafts, thumbnail options, and platform format notes.

### **Athlete Content Agent**

Prepares athlete post drafts, behind-the-scenes captions, usage reminders, and NILP® review flags.

### **Sponsor Asset Agent**

Prepares sponsor recap copy, branded content notes, partner versions, and delivery package materials.

### **Live Recap Agent**

Prepares event summaries, post-event social copy, fan updates, press notes, and internal recap materials.

### **Broadcast Clip Agent**

Prepares clip notes, rights flags, territory notes, and review materials for broadcast or platform-limited content.

### **Archive Refresh Agent**

Prepares historic clip summaries, player milestone content, season recap notes, and reuse review flags.

### **Internal Handoff Agent**

Prepares structured notes between production, social, brand, athlete relations, legal, sponsor, and delivery owners.
