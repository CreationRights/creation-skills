# **Section 1: The Brand Content Problem**

## **Eyebrow**

THE PROBLEM

## **Headline**

Brand media now moves faster than approval models were built to handle.

## **Body**

Large brands rarely produce one asset for one channel.

A campaign becomes social posts, paid media, product videos, marketplace assets, creator edits, retail copy, PDP content, email graphics, international versions, and refreshed content for future use.

Along the way, teams need to answer practical questions.

Who owns the final claim?  
Who approves talent usage?  
Which assets can be reused?  
Which agent can prepare the first pass?  
Where does legal review enter?  
What must be recorded before paid media runs?  
What should Creation Rights govern?

Creation Skills helps brands design the company process around those decisions.

# **Section 4: Company-Owned Production Agents**

## **Eyebrow**

PRODUCTION AGENTS

## **Headline**

Brand agents need clear jobs, limits, and approval paths.

## **Body**

Production agents can support brand teams by preparing drafts, versions, checklists, captions, product copy, campaign variations, and delivery materials.

But they need rules before they support real media.

A brand production agent should know what it can prepare, what it cannot decide, who owns the output, and when a human must approve.

## **Example Agents**

### **Product Media Agent**

Prepares product video outlines, image briefs, feature breakdowns, marketplace copy, and product content structures.

### **Social Cutdown Agent**

Prepares short-form suggestions, caption drafts, hook options, platform notes, and social package ideas.

### **Campaign Versioning Agent**

Prepares asset variations, channel copy, format notes, and delivery checklist support.

### **Influencer Asset Prep Agent**

Prepares creator content notes, repost copy, usage reminders, and review materials for human approval.

### **Marketplace Content Agent**

Prepares PDP copy, marketplace versions, SKU content, comparison copy, and channel-specific product descriptions.

### **Internal Production Agent**

Supports meeting notes, task routing, file naming, handoff checklists, vendor notes, and production status updates.

## **Body**

Brand assets are rarely used once.

A campaign image becomes a paid ad.  
A creator video becomes a social cutdown.  
A product video becomes a marketplace asset.  
A launch asset becomes a seasonal refresh.  
A product claim gets reused in a new channel.  
A talent image gets placed into a new format.

Creation Skills helps brands define when reuse is allowed, when review is required, and what needs to be recorded.

## **Reuse Questions**

Was NILP® present?

What channel was approved?

What territory was approved?

Was paid media included?
