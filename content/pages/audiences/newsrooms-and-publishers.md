# **Section 1: The Editorial Production Problem**

## **Eyebrow**

THE PROBLEM

## **Headline**

Publishing now creates more media around every story.

## **Body**

Newsrooms and publishers are no longer only producing one article or one issue.

A story may need headlines, summaries, captions, image packages, social cutdowns, newsletters, homepage copy, search copy, push alerts, video scripts, audio notes, archive context, market versions, and syndication materials.

The production path can become scattered.

Source material changes.  
Headlines move quickly.  
Images need rights review.  
Archive material gets reused.  
Social posts need approval.  
Legal or standards review may be required.  
Agents may prepare summaries, captions, drafts, and packages.  
Records need to show what was approved before publication.

Creation Skills helps fix the company flow around editorial media.

# **Section 4: Company-Owned Production Agents**

## **Eyebrow**

PRODUCTION AGENTS

## **Headline**

Editorial agents need source context, limits, and approval paths.

## **Body**

Production agents can help publishing teams prepare editorial media faster.

They can summarize source materials, draft captions, prepare headline options, organize archive references, build newsletter summaries, clean transcripts, prepare social packages, and check delivery materials.

But they need rules before they support real editorial media.

A production agent should know what it can prepare, what it cannot decide, who owns the output, and when a human must approve.

## **Example Agents**

### **Story Summary Agent**

Prepares structured summaries, key points, source notes, and editor-ready briefing material from approved inputs.

### **Headline Options Agent**

Prepares headline options, dek options, social headlines, and search-friendly variations for editor review.

### **Image Caption Agent**

Prepares caption drafts, attribution notes, source references, rights flags, and alt text support.

### **Social Package Agent**

Prepares social copy, quote card options, short-form captions, platform notes, and audience package drafts.

### **Archive Context Agent**

Summarizes older material, flags source gaps, and prepares archive context for editor and rights review.

### **Transcript Cleanup Agent**

Prepares cleaned transcript drafts, speaker notes, timing references, and review-ready materials.

### **Newsletter Prep Agent**

Prepares newsletter summaries, subject line options, section notes, and member update drafts for human approval.

### **Partner Package Agent**

Prepares syndication notes, partner copy, image package requirements, metadata notes, and delivery checklists.
