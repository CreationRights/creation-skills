# **Section 4: Company-Owned Production Agents**

## **Eyebrow**

PRODUCTION AGENTS

## **Headline**

Talent media agents need clear limits before they touch the work.

## **Body**

Production agents can help teams prepare creator and talent-led media faster.

They can summarize creator submissions, prepare caption drafts, check delivery packages, flag missing usage details, organize repost requests, prepare paid media versions, and draft market adaptations.

But they need rules before they support talent-led assets.

A production agent should know what it can prepare, what it cannot decide, who owns the output, and when a human must approve.

## **Example Agents**

### **Creator Submission Agent**

Prepares creator submission summaries, asset notes, missing item flags, and review-ready materials.

### **Talent Usage Check Agent**

Flags whether name, image, likeness, persona, voice, consent, paid media, territory, channel, or usage window review may be required.

### **Repost Prep Agent**

Prepares repost copy, source notes, permission reminders, and approval materials before brand use.

### **Paid Media Version Agent**

Prepares channel versions, caption drafts, format notes, usage reminders, and review flags before paid placement.

### **Market Version Agent**

Prepares localized copy drafts, territory notes, usage reminders, and approval materials.

### **Creator Delivery Agent**

Checks whether deliverables include required files, captions, thumbnails, metadata, usage notes, and approval records.

### **Talent Campaign Agent**

Prepares campaign asset summaries, talent-facing notes, caption drafts, and delivery package materials.
